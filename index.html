How to download Macnote.

1

Unzip the zip file

Unzip the file that you downloaded.

2

Moving Macnote

After unziping you should get the Macnote app, Now move it to applications folder.

3

Opening Macnote

Now open Macnote and a popup will apear click cancel on the popup.

4

Open System Preferences

Now go to System Preferences->Security & Privacy and hit open anyway.

5

Open Macnote again

Now open Macnote again and click open.
